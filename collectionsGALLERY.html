STRUCTURES IN NET

Usually my work starts by looking inside myself and then outside from the inside. From the maze of questions and impressions in the multidimensional world we are living in, I see patterns, structures, reproductions being systematically repeated. Words, actions, behaviours, thoughts.

Looking at nature, humans, a cell, renewing and perishing, renewing and perishing endlessly. Full of wonder I quiet my mind listens to the language of the heart. I surrender like a tree, with roots into the earth and with the crown up in the sky.

With Structures in Net I’m creating a new alphabet a new and unknown language. It’s soundless but will be felt when silence enters.
